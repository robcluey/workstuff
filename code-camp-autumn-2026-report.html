Key Insights

Watch — Revenue

Season down 9.7% vs Autumn 2025

Total revenue of $961K is the weakest season in the dataset. Gap vs prior Autumn is $103K. All channels contracted except schools and Bing.

Watch — Paid Search

Google Branded declining

Branded search revenue fell 14% vs Autumn 2025 (-$29K). Likely signals reduced organic brand demand or competitor bidding on brand terms. Unbranded held relatively well (+8% relative to season).

Watch — State

VIC significant underperformance

Victoria declined 22% vs Autumn 2025 ($-70K) and lost 4 percentage points of revenue share (30% → 26%). Largest absolute contributor to overall revenue shortfall.

Watch — CRM

Email and CRM revenue declining

Email down 14% vs average (-$18K); CRM overall down 12% (-$22K). May indicate list fatigue, deliverability issues, or reduced re-booking rates from prior cohorts.

Win — Schools

Schools promo codes outperforming

Schools (promo codes) up 36% vs Autumn 2025 and +31% vs the prior-season average. Revenue of $44.6K is the highest in the dataset for this channel. Clear growth lever.

Win — Schools

Schools (Ash) growing

Ash schools channel delivered $67K — up 12% vs Autumn 2025 and +17% relative to the overall season. Combined with promo codes, schools channels are the clear bright spot.

Win — State

SA growing off a small base

South Australia up 35% vs Autumn 2025 to $37.1K. Small absolute number but consistent growth across all three prior seasons. May warrant additional investment.

Note — Pacing

Late-season surge in weeks 8–9

Week 8 delivered $73.1K vs $30.2K in Autumn 2025 — a 142% uplift. Suggests bookings shifted later in the campaign window. Worth monitoring if this pattern continues into Winter 2026.

Note — Channel

Unknown/Direct holds share at 45%

Despite falling $32K in absolute terms, direct/unknown revenue is tracking with the overall season decline (-7% vs avg). Attribution gap remains the largest single "channel" and masks true performance.
